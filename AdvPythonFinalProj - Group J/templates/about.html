About

This project was the dual responsibility of Nick Aquisto and Peter Majors, both seniors at Fordham's Gabelli School of Business. It was our final project for Professor Daniel Groner's Advanced Python For Business course, which required us to use much of the techniques learned over the course of the semester.

Our original intentions were to design a simple terminal-based program to compare the performance of two stocks in the S&P500 and then calculate a "similarity score" based on their data. Thanks to yFinance and Flask, we were able to develop a frontend that pulls live stock data and creates comprehensive reports for users.
